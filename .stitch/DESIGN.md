## Creative Direction

Festival Companion should feel like a cinematic concierge for festival travel:

- dark, atmospheric, and editorial rather than neon or generic SaaS
- precise and data-literate without looking clinical
- premium, soft-surfaced, and slightly asymmetrical

## Core Tokens

- Background: `#131313`
- Surface low: `#1C1B1B`
- Surface high: `#2A2A2A`
- Surface highest: `#353534`
- Primary sand: `#F1E0C3`
- Primary container: `#D4C4A8`
- Secondary plum: `#4B3F72`
- Tertiary teal: `#A8CCD6`
- Body text: `#E5E2E1`
- Muted text: `#CEC5B9`

## Typography

- Headings: Newsreader
- Body / labels: Manrope
- Data: IBM Plex Mono

## Interaction Rules

- Prefer tonal separation over hard borders
- Use rounded capsule inputs and controls
- Use glassmorphism selectively for sticky or floating surfaces
- Keep accent color usage sparse and intentional
- Confidence badges should lean teal
- Recommendation badges should lean sand

## Layout Rules

- Large editorial headlines with tight leading
- Generous whitespace
- Asymmetrical hero composition where helpful
- Cards should feel soft and layered, not boxed in
